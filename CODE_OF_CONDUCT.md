# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

By participating, you agree to uphold its terms. We expect contributors to be respectful, constructive, and welcoming.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience behaviour that breaks this code, please email **3dresources@devanshutak.xyz**.

Reports are handled privately and in good faith. Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, commits, issues, or contributors who do not follow the code.
